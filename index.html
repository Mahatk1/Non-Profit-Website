About Us About

Our Youth

We believe all youth can embrace who they are, define their future, and change the world — regardless of their background.

Our Vision

To create a thriving community where every child has equal opportunities to succeed. We envision immigrant and low-income youth in South King County empowered through sports and education — becoming leaders who make lasting change.

Our Mission

To empower immigrant and low-income youth through our community basketball league — providing a safe after-school program that promotes academic achievement, leadership development, and community engagement.
